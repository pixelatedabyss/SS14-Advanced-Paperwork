[color=#1b67a5]This form is used to issue an official execution order aboard the station under the sole authority of the Captain, or in the Captain’s absence, the Head of Personnel. It is intended for cases where an individual has been sentenced to capital punishment in accordance with station law and approved judicial or disciplinary procedures.

All sections of this form must be completed accurately, including the identity of the individual, the approved justification for execution, and the authorizing signature of the Captain or Head of Personnel. Execution orders are subject to immediate review and may only be carried out by designated Security personnel. Misuse or falsification of this form is considered a severe breach of protocol and may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

Please provide the crew member’s current name, species, and the desired name.
